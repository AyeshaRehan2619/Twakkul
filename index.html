Popular Surah of the Quran with their fazilat (benefits)

Here is the collection of the most poweful surah of the Quran Shareef, and it is said that memorising these surah are considered as sawab (good deed)

Surah Yaseen

It is often referred as "Heart Of the Quran". This Surah emphasis on the afterlife and oneness of Allah SWT.

Surah Al-Fateha

It is often considered as the mother of the Quran. It emphsizes on Allah's attribute of mercy and judgement.

Surah Mulk

It emphasizes on Allah's sovereignty and creation, urging reflection on Allah's power and majesty.

Surah Kahf

It offers guidance on faith, perseverance, and trails of the life. The surah highlights the significance of gratitude for Allah's blessings.

Surah Kausar

It is the shortest suran with only three verses. The surah offers solace and reassurance to the Prophet.It emphasizes on the importance of devotation and gratitude.

Surah Baqarah

It is the second longest surah of the Quran, is known for its comprehensive teachings on faith, guidance, and social and legal aspects of life.
